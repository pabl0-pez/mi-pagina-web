This project was conceptualized around birth, both physical and existential. The silhouette’s bulky shapes emulate those of babies at the moment of delivery. The textile is made with a synthetic material layered together to symbolize the layering that occurs through the acquirement of experience, knowledge, and consciousness. The colors are the three primary colors in terms of light (RGB) symbolizing enlightenment.
